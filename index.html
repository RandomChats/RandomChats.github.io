Hello!

My name is Nova McLaughlin, and I'm a Software Developer and Unity Developer. I am a second-year Boise State University student currently working toward my Bachelor's Degree with the Games Interactive Media and Mobile major.

Since I was young, I always had dreamed of working with Video Games in some shape or form. As I grew older, I experimented with many facets of how to contribute to making Video Games, and I found that I had a passion for working with the programming and narrative sides of development. Since then, I have begun to learn as much as I could about programming, both with Game Design, as well as the uses languages have on their own. I specifically focused on learning the Unity Engine, initially as a foundation, but that then turned into wanting to master the engine, so I can apply my skills to other game engines in the future.

I have been using Unity for 4 years and am knowledgeable of the Unity Engine as well as the specific use of C#, and the differences between C# itself and C# within Unity. I have also been using Java for 3 years, developing programs to help me gather a better understanding of Object-Oriented Programming and common principles that are used within different OOP languages. In addition to this, I also have 5 years of HTML, CSS, and Javascript experience, and learned how to use libraries such as jQuery, Node.js, and React. I also used PHP for back-end web development in conjunction with MySQL for database design and management.
